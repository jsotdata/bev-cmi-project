# How to duplicate objects around a circle

Inkscape-1.2

## Steps

Select circle icon.
Adjust the arc angle, e.g., -10 deg and +10 deg.
Hold Ctrl, and click and drag to make a pie.

Adjust the snapping setup
before moving the rotation center
in the next step.

Move the rotation center to the center of pie's circle.

Duplicate the pie (Ctrl-d).

Object > Transform > Rotate.

Repeat duplicate and rotate.
